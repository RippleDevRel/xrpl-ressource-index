## Bug Bounty

Ripple runs multiple private bug bounty programs in partnership with **Bugcrowd**. Participation is by invitation only.

### How to Request an Invitation

Email **bugs@ripple.com** with:
1. Your Bugcrowd handle or the email address on your Bugcrowd account
2. Which program(s) you want to join (see table below)

### Available Programs

| # | Program | Scope | Expertise Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Ripple Bug Bounty Program](https://ripple.com/legal/bug-bounty) | External assets & Ripple Payment Products | API, Web |
| 2 | Ripple XRP Ledger Managed Bug Bounty | xrpld, Clio, xrpl.js, xrpl-py, xrpl4j | Blockchain, Web3 |
| 3 | RLUSD Contracts Bug Bounty | RLUSD stablecoin contracts | Blockchain, Web3 |
| 4 | XRPL EVM Sidechain Managed Bug Bounty | XRPL EVM Sidechain | Blockchain, Web3 |

> Once invited, review the rules of engagement and policies carefully before submitting any reports.

### Legal Note
Reports made under the bug bounty program are treated with strict confidence. Ripple reserves the right to take legal action against abuse of the program or any illegal activity.
